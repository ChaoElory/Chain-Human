摘  要：在数字技术飞速发展的当下，数字化转型深度融入企业生产经营，日益发挥改
善企业资源配置和增强企业市场竞争力的重要作用。本文利用2007—2022 年中国A 股上市
公司面板数据进行实证分析，深入剖析企业数字化转型对上游供应商企业绿色全要素生产率
的影响与机制。研究发现，企业数字化转型能显著促进上游供应商绿色全要素生产率的增长，
即产生供应链上游绿色溢出效应，其作用机制是降低上游供应商的交易成本和提升其内部控
制水平。异质性检验表明，该绿色溢出效应在以下情境中更为显著：上下游企业均在东部地
区；下游企业属于非技术密集型行业或上游供应商属于技术密集型行业；下游企业属于非高
污染行业或上游供应商属于高污染行业；上下游企业均非国有；上下游企业均规模较大。本
文从供应链视角出发，拓展了企业绿色全要素生产率的理论框架，为政策制定者在增强企业
竞争力、促进经济绿色转型与实现可持续发展方面提供了重要的理论支撑与实践指引。
关键词：企业数字化转型；绿色全要素生产率；供应链；绿色溢出效应

色发展合作平台，促进技术先进企业与技术水平较低企业之间的知识转移和技术合作。

Abstract ：With the rapid development of digital technology, digital
transformation has deeply integrated into the corporate production and operation
processes and has become a key driving force for reorganizing resources, reshaping
enterprise structures, and changing the pattern of market competition. This paper
conducts an empirical analysis using the panel data of Chinese A-share listed
companies from 2007 to 2022, deeply analyzing the impact and mechanism of enterprise
digital transformation on the green total factor productivity of upstream supplier
enterprises. The results indicate that enterprise digital transformation
significantly promotes the green total factor productivity of upstream supplier
enterprises, generating an upstream supply chain green spillover effect. The
mechanism is to reduce the transaction costs of upstream suppliers and stimulate
their internal control levels. Heterogeneity tests show that the upstream supply
chain green spillover effect of enterprise digital transformation is more
significant when both upstream and downstream enterprises are located in the eastern
region, in non-technology-intensive downstream enterprises or technology-intensive
upstream enterprises, when downstream enterprises are in non-high-pollution
industries or upstream enterprises are in high-pollution industries, in
non-state-owned enterprises, and in large enterprises. This paper expands the
theoretical framework of enterprise green total factor productivity from the
perspective of the supply chain and provides important theoretical support and
practical guidance for policymakers in enhancing enterprise competitiveness,
promoting economic green transformation, and achieving sustainable development.
Keywords：Enterprise Digital Transformation；Green Total Factor Productivity；
