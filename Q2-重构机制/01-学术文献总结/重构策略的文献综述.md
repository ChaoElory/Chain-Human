### 方向一：供应链重构策略的分类学（Taxonomy）

| 策略类型 | 学术关键词 | 建议检索方向 |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **备份策略（Backup Sourcing）** | Dual sourcing, multi-sourcing, supply base redundancy | 运营管理期刊（JOM, POM, IJOPM） |
| **搜寻策略（Search & Match）** | Supply network search, supplier discovery, inter-organizational search | 组织理论（ASQ, Org Science） |
| **整合策略（Vertical Integration）** | Vertical integration, backward integration, make-or-buy decision | Transaction Cost Economics（JLE, SMJ） |
| **关系策略（Relational Governance）** | Relational contracting, long-term buyer-supplier relationship | Supply chain management（JSCM, IJPDLM） |
